about us

We are a Johannesburg based business that delivers cannabis to your door. you can place orders by calling us or booking on our website.we will deliver within 2 hours and you can pay securely online .







How it works
1. Browse
Grandpa's has the most trusted and comprehensive list of marijuana delivery and pickup ,options in your area. With hundreds of listings and 10x as many products, you can easily search to find our favorites or browse to try something new from your chosen dispensary.When you find a menu item you want, simply tap to add it to your bag.
2. Order
When you’re ready to check out, tap on the shopping bag. You can review, customize,and when everything looks good, submit your order to your chosen dispensary. Securely save your Medical Recommendation and identification cards for even faster checkout.
3. Track
Follow along as your order is processed by your chosen dispensary.You’ll receive real-time notifications when your order is on its way to your door or ready for pickup in store.
